Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a spiced tomato-based gravy.

Ingredients

For the Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp cashew paste (blend soaked cashews with water)
  • Salt to taste

For the Paneer:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 tsp butter (for sautéing, optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Base:

    1. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, and cardamom. Sauté for a few seconds.
    2. Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
    3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.

  2. Make the Gravy:

    1. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
    2. Stir in red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
    3. Add cashew paste and mix well. Cook for another 2 minutes.
    4. Add fresh cream and mix. Simmer for a minute.

  3. Add Paneer:

    1. (Optional) Lightly sauté paneer cubes in butter for a richer taste.
    2. Add paneer cubes to the gravy and mix well.

  4. Final Touch:

    1. Sprinkle garam masala and crushed kasuri methi. Mix well.
    2. Simmer for 2 minutes on low heat.

  5. Serve:

    1. Garnish with fresh cream or coriander leaves.
    2. Serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice.
